About Us

ResQFood, our Food Waste Reduction Webapp, is a mission-driven platform fostering local initiatives to eradicate hunger in India. Empowering compassionate citizens, ResQFood facilitates easy creation and management of impactful projects, encourages community collaboration, offers real-time impact tracking, provides educational resources, seamless donation integration, and timely notifications. Join the ResQFood community today and contribute to building a hunger-free nation through technology and compassion.
